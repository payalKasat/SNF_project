Social Networking Forum

While social media was used to spread religious and political unrest on a large scale, Networking For Social Cause" movement was founded by Pramod Gopalrao Gaikwad in the year 2010 on Facebook. This informal movement was later on instituionalised as 'Social Networking Forum'. The movement has received great response from the social net-workers across the world and through this initiative, hundreds of projects are undertaken and implemented.

Success Stories...

More than 300 social activities are carried out across the state of Maharashtra and other states in India with the help of young social networkers on social media. To name the few are…

  • 10 Villages are made water self sufficient in two years.
  • 10 tribal schools are converted into digital school.
  • 12000 trees planted in various districts of the state.
  • Books\notebooks\Education kits are made available to schools in tribal areas.
  • 6000 blood bottles collected so far. Yearly two blood donation camps are conducted in various cities.
  • 12 cleft surgeries are performed on poor children.
  • Many orphanages \ old age homes are provided with eatables and daily needs.
  • Conducted many medical camps of cancer detection for poor women. 500 women are benefited till date.

SHAHID JAWAN FUND

The fund aims towards providing monetary relief to the families of soldiers who lost their lives during service to the nation. The fundraising started in 2013 to imbibe the importance of patriotism in the young generation of India through social media.

RURAL & TRIBAL DEVELOPMENT

In spite of ample qualities and hardworking nature people in rural and tribal areas lack behind just because of availability of opportunities. Social networking forum is working on creating such opportunities by supporting them in education, health and legal sector.

ENVIROMENTAL CONSERVATION

The problem of degrading forest cover and increasing pollution has been addressed by the social networking forum by organizing tree plantation drives. The forum began handling larger projects like mass afforestation, river pollution issues and several others of its kind.

OUR VISION

  • To motivate youngsters on Social Networking Sites for social causes.
  • To help Orphanages, old age homes and families of Martyred soldiers, with the active participation of youngsters on Social Media Sites.
  • To stop/minimize cyber crimes on social media.

OUR MISION

  • Mission of ‘Social Networking for social cause’ movement is to create responsible next generation of India through social Networking.
